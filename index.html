01 — Sealing Solutions

Seals that disappear, performance that doesn't.

Curated from leading global manufacturers and specified to the millimetre for aluminium, uPVC, wooden and metal doors — the seals we supply deliver fire containment, acoustic isolation and weather-tight performance without compromising the architecture.

Fire Seals

Intumescent and smoke seals tested to international fire standards. Up to 120 minutes of integrity for compartmented buildings.

  • EN 1634-1
  • Smoke + Cold
  • 30 / 60 / 120 min

Acoustic Seals

High-attenuation perimeter and meeting-stile seals that dampen airborne and impact sound — verified to lab-tested STC and Rw values.

  • Up to 54 dB
  • Rw / STC tested
  • Studio grade

Drop Seals

Automatic and semi-automatic threshold seals that deploy on door close — sealing the gap below without scraping floors.

  • Concealed mortise
  • Surface mount
  • Heavy duty

Perimeter Seals

Continuous head, jamb and meeting-stile sealing for double, single and rebated doors. Discreet finishes from anodized to PVD.

  • Aluminium carrier
  • EPDM / Silicone
  • Custom RAL

Sliding & Pivot Customisation

Bespoke sealing geometries for sliding doors, telescopic systems and oversized pivots — sourced and customised to your panel weight, swing and finish.

  • Up to 600 kg leaf
  • Pivot & sliding
  • Made to order

All Substrates

One trusted partner across material categories — aluminium, uPVC, hardwood, softwood and metal — with consistent performance from a single point of accountability.

  • Aluminium
  • uPVC
  • Wood
  • Metal
